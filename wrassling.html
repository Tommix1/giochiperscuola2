Description

Step into the ring and unleash chaotic wrestling action in Wrassling! This retro-style game puts you in control of eccentric wrestlers as they grapple and tumble their way to victory. With simple controls and exaggerated physics, prepare for hilarious and unpredictable matches that will keep you entertained for hours. Whether you're battling against the computer or challenging a friend in multiplayer mode, Wrassling promises non-stop fun and excitement.

Controls:

Press the left and right arrow keys to move your wrestler.
Tap the up arrow key to jump and hold it to perform a high jump.
Use the X key to extend your wrestler's arms and grab opponents.
Press the Z key to perform a quick punch or throw an opponent.

Tips and tricks:

  • Master the timing of your jumps to avoid getting thrown out of the ring.
  • Grab and toss your opponents to gain the upper hand in matches.
  • Keep an eye out for power-ups that appear randomly in the ring to gain temporary advantages.

About the creator:

Wrassling is developed by Colin Lane Games, known for their quirky and addictive arcade-style games.
