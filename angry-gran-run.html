Description

Join the adventure of Angry Gran Run, where you step into the shoes of the feisty grandma on a wild escapade! Help Granny escape from the asylum she's been confined to and guide her through the bustling streets of the city. Dodge obstacles, leap over barriers, and swipe past bystanders as you dash through the vibrant urban landscape. Collect coins along the way to unlock power-ups and customize Granny's appearance. Can you lead Granny to freedom?

Controls:

Swipe left or right to change lanes.
Swipe up to jump over obstacles.
Swipe down to slide under barriers.
Tilt your device left or right to steer Granny.
Tap the screen to activate power-ups when available.

Tips and tricks:

  • Keep an eye out for power-ups and use them strategically to overcome difficult obstacles.
  • Collect as many coins as possible to unlock new outfits and upgrades for Granny.
  • Stay focused and react quickly to sudden obstacles to maintain your momentum.
